Master Data Management

EarthKraft’s Master Data Management solutions help you unify, clean, and standardize data across systems—ensuring accuracy, consistency, and control. Whether it's customer, product, or financial data, our MDM framework empowers businesses to make confident, data-driven decisions.

Virtual Data Pipeline

Our virtual data pipeline technology connects and transforms your data in real-time without needing to copy or move it. EarthKraft enables seamless data access across sources, reduces latency, and increases agility for analytics, AI, and cloud migrations

EarthKraft’s OneView

OneView is EarthKraft’s intelligent data visualization and monitoring platform that brings together metrics from diverse sources into a single, actionable view. Get real-time insights, alerts, and performance tracking across your business landscape—all in one place.

Big Data & Analytics

We offer big data solutions that are built to handle large-scale, fast-moving data. Using advanced analytics and AI models, EarthKraft helps you uncover patterns, predict trends, and derive measurable business value from structured and unstructured data.

Datastage Training

Upskill your team with our professional Datastage training programs. From ETL fundamentals to advanced job design, EarthKraft’s hands-on training helps professionals master IBM DataStage and integrate it into enterprise-level data operations.

Outsourcing

Our expert teams manage everything from data engineering to AI model development, ensuring cost-effective delivery, faster timelines, and high-quality results tailored to your business needs.
